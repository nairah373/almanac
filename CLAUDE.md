# Product Name

[Temporary Name Placeholder]

A premium academic marketplace for Indian college students where trusted students, creators, clubs, and coaching centers can upload, discover, and monetize high-quality study materials.

The platform is designed around trust, academic prestige, calm minimalism, and intelligent discovery.

This is NOT a generic EdTech website.
This product should feel premium, focused, modern, and academically elite.

---

# Core Product Vision

Build India's most trusted academic resource platform for engineering and MBBS students.

Students should be able to:
- discover high-quality notes instantly
- trust the authenticity of resources
- learn from top-performing students
- feel part of an intelligent academic community
- access study materials in a calm and distraction-free environment

The platform should emotionally feel like:
- a premium academic library
- a smart student ecosystem
- a trustworthy study companion

Avoid building a noisy, colorful, dopamine-heavy EdTech product.
